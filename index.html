Microbial Ecology and Evolution

Hi, I'm Ford Fishman. Currently, I am a PhD student at Indiana University in the Lennon Lab. I'm primarily interested in how the coevolution between bacteria and their viruses is impacted by horizontal gene transfer spatial and temporal separation. At the moment, I am primarily using computational modeling to study these interactions, but I am hoping to soon test the results of my models in the wet lab.
